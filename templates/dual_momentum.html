About This Strategy

Logic:

  • Compute the 12-month return for VFIAX (US S&P 500) and VTIAX (international equity), and the 12-month rolling return on 3-month T-bills (FRED DGS3MO).
  • An equity sleeve is "passing" if its 12-month ROC is above the T-bill hurdle.
  • Both passing → hold the one with the higher 12-month ROC.
  • One passing → hold that one.
  • Neither passing → hold AGG (US aggregate bonds) as the defensive sleeve.

Rebalance: Monthly on the last trading day.

Historical proxy splices: VFIAX is extended back through VFINX (pre-2000-11-13); VTIAX through VGTSX (pre-1999-08-17) and VTMGX (1999-08-17 → 2010-11-28); AGG through VBMFX (pre-2003-09-22).
